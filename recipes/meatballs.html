Meatball recipe

Description of recipe:

What a great addition to the other 2 recipes

these will surely be a great addition to any italian meal

All props go to allrecipes again

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen cooked meatballs
  • 1 can cranberry sauce
  • 1 bottle tomato-based chili
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 drops of tobasco sauce

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in slowcooker
  2. cook on high until meatballs are thawed
  3. serve and eat
